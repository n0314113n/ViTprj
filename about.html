This website is a labor of love.

It is also a coping mechanism.
It is an attempt to heal and reconcile the fragmented pieces of my brother that schizophrenia leaves behind.
Above all, it is a tribute to my brother, as he lives and breathes and bravely battles the persistent, malicious voices.

schiz·o·phre·ni·a /ˌskitsəˈfrēnēə,ˌskitsəˈfrenēə/ noun PSYCHIATRY

Schizophrenia a long-term mental disorder of a type involving a breakdown in the relation between thought, emotion, and behavior, leading to faulty perception, inappropriate actions and feelings, withdrawal from reality and personal relationships into fantasy and delusion, and a sense of mental fragmentation.




This guy playing guitar in the middle of the road in the dark of night is my brother. He has schizoaffective disorder,
which is a fancy way of saying he has a double diagnosis of schizophrenia and a mood disorder (bipolar II).
